Notes on Never Let Me Go

Setting Characters Themes Important Moments Questions Additional Sections

Setting

Hailsham, a boarding school in England, and later the Cottages where students transition to adulthood, are central locations that reflect the isolation and control over the characters' lives.

Characters

Kathy H., the narrator, Tommy, Ruth, and the guardians at Hailsham are key figures shaping the story.

Themes

Themes include identity, mortality, friendship, and the ethical dilemmas of cloning.

Important Moments

The revelation of their purpose as clones and Kathy's reflections on her time at Hailsham are pivotal moments.

Questions

What does it mean to live a meaningful life? How do the characters grapple with their pre-determined destinies?
